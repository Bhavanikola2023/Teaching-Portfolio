About Me

As an AI enthusiast with a zeal for transforming education through technology, I leverage my 24 years of math instruction to champion AI literacy. Currently pursuing my Ed.D. in Educational/Instructional Technology, I craft presentations that resonate and enlighten audiences about the potential of AI in education.

My work demystifies AI, fostering an inclusive understanding that empowers educators and students alike. Leading by example, I chair an education foundation, tirelessly working to ensure access to quality education is matched by cutting-edge AI integration.

As the Founder and CEO of Eclatech USA LLC, I deliver cutting-edge AI training, workshops, certifications, and engaging speaking engagements that inspire action and unlock potential. My journey began with curiosity and a passion for technology, which has evolved into a mission to bridge the AI gap and transform lives through education and innovation.
